TOPIA Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: 03 June 2026

TOPIA (Tourism Platform Intelligence Assistant) allows users to request deletion of their account and associated profile information.

How to Delete Your Account

Users can delete their account directly from the TOPIA mobile application:

  1. Open TOPIA.
  2. Navigate to Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Data Removed

When account deletion is completed, TOPIA may remove:

  • User authentication account.
  • Profile information.
  • Display name.
  • Contact information.
  • Location information.
  • Organization information.
  • Profile photo references.

Data Retention

Certain records may be temporarily retained when required for security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or system integrity purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are generally processed immediately or within a reasonable technical processing period.

Need Assistance?

If you experience issues deleting your account, please contact the application administrator through the official support channels available within TOPIA.
