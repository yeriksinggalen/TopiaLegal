TOPIA Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-03

TOPIA is a tourism intelligence application designed to support destination services, local tourism providers, tourism research, and tourism decision-support workflows.

Information We Collect

TOPIA may collect account and profile information voluntarily provided by users, including name or display name, email address, phone number, location or service region, organization or business name, account role, and profile photo reference when provided.

How We Use Information

Information is used to authenticate users, manage user profiles, support tourism-related workflows, personalize account features, and maintain application security and reliability.

Data Storage

TOPIA uses Firebase services for authentication and profile data management. Profile data is stored for application functionality and account management purposes.

Data Sharing

TOPIA does not sell user personal data. Data may be processed only through application infrastructure required to operate the service.

Account Deletion

Users may initiate account deletion through the application when signed in. Account deletion may remove the user profile and authentication account associated with TOPIA.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as TOPIA develops new features, services, or compliance requirements.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please use the official support channel provided by the TOPIA application owner.
